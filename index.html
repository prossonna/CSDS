Welcome to Centre for Sustainable Development Studies Pvt. Ltd

Centre for Sustainable Development Studies Pvt. Ltd. (CSDS) is a consulting company registered with the Government of Nepal and led by Nepali development professionals providing expertise and professional services to contribute in strengthening Local Governments and bringing change in development process of Nepal. CSDS is providing specialize expertise in Local Level Planning, Local Level Information Management System, independent programme reviews, monitoring and evaluation, programme design and associated policy development, rural livelihoods improvement, renewable energy. In providing its services, CSDS is committed to provide the highest standards of professionalism, integrity and quality assurance to all assignments it undertakes.
